# Snapshot Sweep Report Style

## Required Sections

Korean reports should use these sections in this order:

1. 요약 결론
2. 시나리오 비교
3. 핵심 민감도 해석
4. 시나리오별 조정 후보
5. 위험 집중도
6. 데이터 품질
7. 종목별 평가 및 추천 액션
8. 액션 우선순위
9. IPS 해석
10. 한눈에 읽기용 문장

## Interpretation Rules

- Underweight core assets: prefer regular purchase increase or allocation redirection, not immediate lump-sum buying.
- Overweight or high-risk satellite assets: prefer reducing/pausing new purchases, thesis review, simplification review, or hold/observe before considering a sale.
- Low efficiency alone is not a sell reason. Combine it with risk excess, thesis status, overlap, and portfolio complexity.
- Low-quality data means hold, observe, or verify data. Do not convert unreliable rows into buy/sell recommendations.
- Drawdown contexts should reinforce core-first regular purchase behavior, not broad dip-buying.
- If a ticker appears frequently across scenarios, call it a persistent review signal, not an automatic trade.

## One-Glance Sentence

Write 2-4 Korean sentences that answer:

- Is the portfolio broadly healthy or review-needed?
- Which exposures need regular purchase increase, reduction/pause, or thesis review?
- Which rows are data-quality holds?
- What should not be overinterpreted as immediate trading?

Avoid detailed tables or new numbers in the one-glance sentence unless they materially change the conclusion.
